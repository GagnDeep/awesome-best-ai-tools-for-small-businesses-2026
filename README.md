Contributions are welcome! Please read these guidelines before submitting:

### How to Contribute

1. **Fork the repository** on GitHub.
2. **Create a new branch** for your changes: `git checkout -b add-new-tool` or `git checkout -b fix-broken-link`.
3. **Make your changes** — add a tool, fix a link, improve formatting.
4. **Test your changes** — verify all links work and markdown renders correctly.
5. **Commit** with a clear message: `git commit -m "Add [Tool Name] in [Category]"`.
6. **Push** to your fork: `git push origin add-new-tool`.
7. **Open a Pull Request** against the `main` branch.

### Contribution Criteria

To be added to this list, a tool must:

- Be **directly relevant to small businesses** (1–50 employees, solopreneurs, freelancers)
- Have a **free tier or affordable pricing** (no enterprise-only tools)
- Offer **AI-powered features** that solve a real business problem
- Be **actively maintained** (no abandoned or deprecated tools)
- Include a valid, working **URL** to the official website or GitHub repo

### Style Guide for Tool Entries

```
### Tool Name

**Website:** [https://example.com](https://example.com)

**Tags:** `category` `subcategory` `feature`

Description (80–150 words) covering what the tool does, who it's for,
why it's valuable for small businesses, and any notable free or
affordable pricing details. End with a sentence about what makes it
stand out.
```

### What NOT to Submit

- Tools without AI features (we're specifically an *AI* tools list)
- Enterprise-only software with no free or SMB-accessible tier
- Generic developer tools (IDEs, version control, etc.) unless they have a specific SMB product
- Duplicate entries for tools already listed
- Tools with broken or inaccessible URLs

## FAQ

### What are the best AI tools for small businesses on a budget?

The best free AI tools for small businesses include **Wave** for accounting and invoicing, **Google Analytics 4** for website analytics, **Tidio** for customer service chatbots, **Notion AI** for productivity, **Calendly** for scheduling, and **Microsoft Clarity** for UX analytics. These platforms offer genuine AI-powered features at no cost, making them ideal for bootstrapped businesses that need to do more with less.

### Are these AI tools suitable for businesses with fewer than 5 employees?

Absolutely. Every tool on this list is chosen specifically because it serves solopreneurs, freelancers, and micro-businesses — not just "small" as in 50-person teams. Platforms like **Wave**, **Chatbase**, **Setmore**, and **Billy** have no minimum seat requirements, while tools like **Notion AI** and **Toggl Track** have generous free tiers that are specifically designed for individual users.

### How do I choose the right AI tool for my small business?

Start by identifying your biggest bottleneck: if customers are asking the same questions repeatedly, a chatbot like **Chatfuel** or **Tidio** will give you the fastest ROI. If you're losing track of invoices and payments, **Wave** or **Zoho Invoice** solves that immediately. If your team spends hours on email, tools like **Lavender** or **Superhuman** reclaim significant time. The best approach is to pick one tool that solves your most painful problem, implement it fully, and then add more AI tools as your business grows.

### Do these AI tools require technical skills to set up?

No. Every tool on this list is designed for non-technical small business owners. Platforms like **Chatbase**, **Tidio**, **Crisp**, and **Calendly** offer drag-and-drop builders and pre-made templates that require zero coding knowledge. Even more technical tools like **Botpress** offer visual builders alongside their code-based options. The average setup time for most tools on this list is under 30 minutes.

### Which AI tools help small businesses get paid faster?

**Wave** and **Zoho Invoice** automate payment reminders and late fee calculations so you don't have to chase clients manually. **PandaDoc** embeds Stripe payment links directly into invoices and proposals. **Nanonets** eliminates the manual data entry that delays invoice processing. **Billy** sends automatic payment follow-ups for overdue invoices. Combined, these tools can reduce your average payment cycle by several days.

### Are there AI tools specifically for small business marketing?

Yes — several on this list. **Jasper** and **Phrasee** handle AI copywriting for ads, emails, and social media. **Instantly** and **Lemlist** automate cold email outreach at scale. **ActiveCampaign** and **ConvertKit** provide AI-assisted email automation and segmentation. **ConvertFlow** personalizes your website experience for each visitor. Together, these tools cover the full small business marketing stack from content creation to campaign analytics.

### What AI tools help with small business hiring?

For small businesses doing their own recruiting, **Manatal** provides a full AI-powered applicant tracking system. **Braintrust** connects you with pre-vetted freelance talent. **Interview AI** helps you run structured, objective interviews with AI-generated questions and candidate evaluations. **Toggl Track** helps you understand where your team's time actually goes during the hiring process. All four have free or affordable tiers designed for small teams.

### How often is this list updated?

This list is reviewed quarterly. We monitor for broken links, deprecated tools, significant pricing changes, and exciting new AI tools that meet our SMB-specific criteria. If you spot anything that needs updating, please open a pull request or issue — community contributions are the fastest way to keep this resource fresh and accurate.
